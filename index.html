Where you learned your skills?

Learning as a beginner was extremely challenging for me, as I had no prior knowledge and no clear direction at the start. I had not begun learning seriously until I explored my college course structure, online learning resources, and conducted my own research. Through this process, I was able to understand the importance of having a proper learning roadmap. Today, I am on a clear and focused path toward becoming a backend developer. I began my journey with the fundamentals of web development, learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Gradually, I transitioned to Java, starting from the basics and progressing through object-oriented programming concepts and advanced Java topics. At present, I am learning MySQL to strengthen my understanding of databases, and after completing this, I plan to move forward with Spring to build robust and scalable backend applications. In the following section, I will share the learning platforms and resources that have guided me throughout each stage of my learning journey.
